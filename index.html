Víctor Toscano Durán

To introduce myself, I am a passionate data scientist with a solid academic background and experience in the field of mathematics and artificial intelligence. I hold a bachelor's degree in Statistics and a master's degree in Logic, Computation, and Artificial Intelligence, supported by years of experience as a Data Scientist. Currently, I am working towards obtaining my PhD in Mathematics("Topological Data Analysis for Trustworthy AI") at the University of Seville, which main goal is to apply Topological Data Analysis (TDA) to enhance the understanding and reliability of neural networks.

Currently, I am immersed in the exciting world of research as part of the European REliable & eXplAinable Swarm Intelligence for People with Reduced mObility (REXASI-PRO) (GRANT-AGREEMENT N0. 101070028) Project at the Department of Applied Mathematics I of the University of Seville. I am an active member of CIMAgroup, a group of researchers where we collaborate on innovative projects and contribute to the advancement of science and technology.

I am based at B2.83, Matemáticas Aplicadas I Department, E.T.S.I. Informática, Universidad de Sevilla, Campus Reina Mercedes, 41015, Sevilla, Spain
